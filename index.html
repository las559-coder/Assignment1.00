Harry Potter

Background

Harry James Potter is the main character in J.K. Rowlings book series. Born on July 31, 1980, he became famous as “The Boy Who Lived” after surviving an attack from Lord Voldemort as a baby. He grew up with his aunt and uncle, the Dursleys, until he discovered he was a wizard at age eleven.

Description

Harry has messy black hair, green eyes, and a lightning-shaped scar on his forehead. He is brave, loyal to his friends, and always willing to stand up for what is right. He plays Quidditch as a Seeker for the Gryffindor team and is known for his quick reflexes. He is also famous in the wizarding world for defeating Lord Voldemort.

Key Events

  1. Discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts.
  2. Defeats the basilisk in the Chamber of Secrets.
  3. Competes in the Triwizard Tournament.
  4. Leads Dumbledores Army.
  5. Defeats Lord Voldemort in the Battle of Hogwarts.

Fun Facts

  • His wand is made of holly and phoenix feather.
  • His Patronus is a stag.
  • He becomes an Auror after Hogwarts.
  • He loves Quidditch and flying.
